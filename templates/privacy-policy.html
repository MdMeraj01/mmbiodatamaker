Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

At Wedding Biodata (weddingbiodata.in), accessible from our website, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Wedding Biodata and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We are a client-side application. This means most of the data you enter (Name, Family details, Photos) stays on your device (browser) and is not uploaded to our servers permanently. We do not sell or share your personal biodata information with third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our website
  • Generate the PDF biodata as requested by you
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our website

3. Local Storage & Cookies

We use local storage technology (like localStorage) solely to store your preferences, such as Dark Mode settings. We do not track your personal movements across other websites.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party libraries (like html2pdf or Google Fonts) to provide functionality. These parties have their own privacy policies regarding how they handle data such as IP addresses.

5. Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information. Since the PDF generation happens in your browser, your sensitive data is generally secure and not stored in our database.

6. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at contact@weddingbiodata.in.
