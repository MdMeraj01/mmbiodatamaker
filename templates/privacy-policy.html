Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

At Wedding Biodata (weddingbiodata.in), one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document explains what information is collected and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We are a client-side application. This means most of the data you enter (such as name, family details, and photos) stays on your device (browser) and is not permanently uploaded to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our website
  • To generate your marriage biodata PDF
  • To improve user experience

3. Local Storage & Cookies

We use browser local storage only to save preferences like dark mode. We do not track users across websites.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools such as Google Fonts or PDF libraries. These services may collect technical data like IP address as per their own policies.

5. Security

Since biodata generation happens directly in your browser, your personal data is generally secure and not stored in our database.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at contact@weddingbiodata.in.
